About Me

I have for the past year been learning software development, recently passing a course run by “Brad Traversy” of Traversy Media and currently in the process of completing a Javascript 2021 Guide with “Maximilian Schwarzmuller”. My aim is to be a full time software developer and excel in both the front and back end.

With over a decade experience in touring the world, I am a hard-working and highly motivated individual who is experienced in both managing live events and assisting as crew. I have excellent communication skills and a positive attitude which have helped in achieving targets, whether it be working to build a stage or communicating with artists, production and venue staff to ensure an event runs successfully.
